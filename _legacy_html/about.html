Our Story

About Hare Krishna Marwar

A sacred journey rooted in devotion and blessed by divine grace

Hare Krishna Marwar is a spiritual organization devoted to uplifting human life by offering opportunities for true happiness, inner peace, good health, and all noble qualities through the practice of God Consciousness.

Inspired by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, the temple is dedicated to spreading Krishna Consciousness and Vedic cultural values in the land of Marwar. Guided by the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, we serve through Annadana Seva, Gau Seva, youth empowerment, cultural festivals, and community outreach.

Aligned with the global Hare Krishna Movement, the temple promotes spiritual growth and social welfare — including Bhagavad Gita study sessions, daily prasadam distribution, youth development programs, and grand festival celebrations.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To spread the timeless teachings of Lord Krishna and provide a spiritual sanctuary where devotees can experience divine love, inner peace, and spiritual growth through the practice of bhakti yoga.

We strive to preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of the Marwar region while fostering global consciousness of Krishna's universal message of love and compassion.

🔭 Our Vision

To become a beacon of spiritual enlightenment in Rajasthan, inspiring thousands to embrace a life of devotion, service, and divine consciousness.

We envision a harmonious community where ancient wisdom meets modern living, creating a world where peace, love, and spiritual prosperity flourish for all beings.

Our Journey

Temple History

A sacred journey that began decades ago, rooted in devotion and blessed by divine grace

2011

Akshaya Patra Kitchen

Launched the mid-day meal program, serving thousands of children daily in Jodhpur.

2017

Chaitanya Kunj

Established the spiritual center for regular programs, festivals, and community gatherings.

2022

Land Sanctioned

Government sanctioned land at Chopasani for the grand temple project.

2023

Temple Construction

Foundation stone laid for the magnificent temple complex — Gupt Govardhan Dham.

"From humble beginnings to a magnificent spiritual center, our temple's history is a testament to the unwavering faith of countless devotees. Each milestone represents the deepening of our community's spiritual roots in the sacred soil of Rajasthan."
Our Inspiration

Srila Prabhupada

"The Hare Krishna movement is not an artificial imposition on the mind. This consciousness is the original, natural energy of the living entity. When we hear the transcendental vibration, this consciousness is revived."

— His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

His Divine Grace Srila Prabhupada is the revered Acharya and Diksha Guru of the Hare Krishna Movement. With unwavering dedication, he sought to enlighten minds, impart spiritual knowledge, and establish Krishna Consciousness across the globe.

Born in 1896 in Calcutta, he traveled to America at the age of 69 and single-handedly started a worldwide spiritual revolution that continues to transform millions of lives today.

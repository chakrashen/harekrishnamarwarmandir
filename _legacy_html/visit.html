Visit the Temple

All are welcome to experience the divine atmosphere of our temple

📍 Address

Hare Krishna Marwar

Chopasani, Near Vastra Mantralay
Jodhpur, Rajasthan
PIN: 342024, India

Nearest Landmark: Vastra Mantralay

🕐 Temple Timings

Daily Opens: 08:30 AM – 08:30 PM

Morning Session: 4:30 AM – 12:30 PM

Evening Session: 4:00 PM – 8:30 PM

Temple closed 12:30 PM – 4:00 PM for deity rest

Visitors are welcome during all aarti timings. Please maintain silence during prayers.

🚗 How to Reach

• By Air: Jodhpur Airport — 15 min drive

• By Train: Jodhpur Junction — 20 min drive

• By Road: On NH-62, well connected to major cities

• Local: Auto-rickshaws and cabs available from station

🏛️ Facilities

• Free Prasadam for all visitors

• Guest house accommodation available

• Ample parking space

• Wheelchair accessible

• Shoe storage available

• Drinking water facilities

What to Expect

👗
Dress Code
Modest, traditional attire preferred. Avoid shorts and sleeveless tops.
📵
Silent Zone
Please keep phones on silent and maintain a peaceful atmosphere.
📸
Photography
Photography allowed in outer areas. Flash photography restricted near deities.
🥿
Footwear
Please remove footwear before entering the temple. Storage is available.

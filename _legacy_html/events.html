Sacred Schedule

Daily Darshan & Aarti Timings

🌅
Mangala Aarti
04:30 AM
Wake up the deity with devotional songs. The first aarti awakens Lord Krishna and welcomes the new day with gratitude.
🌺
Darshan Aarti
07:00 AM
Morning dress and decoration of deities. Adorning the deities with beautiful clothes and ornaments.
🍛
Raj Bhog Aarti
12:00 PM
Midday offering and bhog. The grand afternoon meal offering to the deities.
🛕
Utthapan Aarti
04:00 PM
Afternoon awakening ceremony. Waking the deities from their afternoon rest.
🪔
Sandhya Aarti
07:30 PM
Evening prayers and lamp ceremony. The most popular evening aarti with beautiful lamp offerings.
🌙
Shayan Aarti
08:30 PM
Night rest ceremony. Putting the deities to peaceful rest for the night.

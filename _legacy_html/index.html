About Our Temple

A Magnificent Spiritual Landmark

In the royal city of Jodhpur, renowned for its rich heritage and timeless architecture, Hare Krishna Marwar is envisioned as a magnificent spiritual landmark — a sanctuary of devotion, culture, and divine harmony.

Guided by the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam, we promote devotion, service, and spiritual growth through Annadana Seva, Gau Seva, youth empowerment, and cultural festivals.
